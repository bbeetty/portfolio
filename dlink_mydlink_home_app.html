About the Project

This project was initiated by my PM lead. At the beginning, the company’s technology, cloud, and app teams were developing independently, with little system integration. To provide users with a more intuitive way to manage their smart home devices, we set out to build an app that could connect them all together. In this project, D-Link developed a central Hub that links various devices and introduced the HNAP protocol, enabling seamless communication among three major types of devices — Wi-Fi, Socket, and Z-Wave. My role was to design a user experience that allows people to control their entire smart home with just one phone and one app. This was not just an interface integration, but a large-scale collaboration across technologies and teams. Thanks to the joint efforts of PMs, designers, and engineers, we gradually turned what once seemed impossible into reality.
